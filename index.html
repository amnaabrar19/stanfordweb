About Stanford

Stanford University, officially known as Leland Stanford Junior University, is a prestigious university located in Stanford, California. Founded in 1885 by Leland and Jane Stanford located in the heart of Silicon ValleyStanford has become a hub for groundbreaking discoveries and the home to a diverse community of scholars, scientists, and entrepreneurs..

Admissions at Stanford University

Stanford University offers an exceptional education and a vibrant campus community for students who are passionate about making a difference in the world. The admissions process is highly competitive, with an emphasis on academic excellence, leadership, and a commitment to social impact.

Undergraduate Admissions

Stanford’s undergraduate admissions are holistic, considering not only academic performance but also extracurricular involvement, essays, and letters of recommendation. We encourage students to apply who have demonstrated intellectual curiosity, a desire to contribute to their communities, and leadership potential.

Graduate Admissions

Stanford offers a wide range of graduate programs across various disciplines, and the graduate admissions process is tailored to each school. Graduate applicants are evaluated based on academic achievements, research potential, and a demonstrated commitment to their field of study.

How to Apply

  • Complete the application form online.
  • Submit your academic transcripts and standardized test scores (if required).
  • Provide recommendation letters and personal essays.
  • Attend interviews (if applicable).
